Do you work worldwide even though you’re based in Malaysia?

Yes. I work fully remote and support clients worldwide. For retainers, we align response windows to your timezone and define SLAs clearly.

Do you offer one-time fixes or only monthly plans?

Both. I handle one-time troubleshooting, fixed-scope projects, and monthly retainers. If you’re unsure, start with a one-time assessment and I’ll recommend the best path.

Can you help with cybersecurity?

I support defensive security: IAM/access reviews, endpoint/email security hygiene, and governance-ready reporting. I do not assist with hacking or bypassing security controls.

What do you need from me to start?

A short problem statement, your timezone, your environment (OS/cloud/provider/tools), and your preferred access method. Scope is confirmed before any work begins.
