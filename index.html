FAQ

Fast answers • Clear boundaries
Do you work worldwide even though you’re based in Malaysia?

Yes. We work fully remote and support clients worldwide. For retainers, we align response windows to your timezone and define SLAs clearly.

Do you offer one-time fixes or only monthly plans?

Both. We handle one-time troubleshooting, fixed-scope projects, and monthly retainers. If you’re unsure, start with a one-time assessment and we’ll recommend the best path.

Can you help with cybersecurity?

We support defensive security: IAM/access audits, endpoint/email security hygiene, log review support, and governance-ready reporting. We do not assist with hacking or bypassing security controls.

What do you need from us to start?

A short problem statement, your timezone, your environment (OS/cloud/provider/tools), and your preferred access method. We confirm scope before any work begins.
